Know Your Map

The colors of the tiles in the Heat Map represent the measured expression value of a given gene in a given experiment. By default, a three color scale is used with blue indicating low expression values, yellow indicating intermediately expressed genes, and red representing highly expressed genes.

HeatMap contain different indication colors to show the range of population.
